How do I apply for the NHS Low Income Scheme?

To apply for help with your health costs via the NHS Low Income Scheme, you need to complete, and send in, an HC1 application form; we cannot accept a faxed or emailed version.

All parts of the HC1 must be completed, unless instructions within the form tell you otherwise.

You may be eligible to apply online instead, however, you cannot apply over the telephone.

Ireland

If you're currently living in Northern Ireland you need to contact your local Social Security office with regards to your HC1 application form.

If you're a student from Northern Ireland who's living in England, Wales or Scotland, you need to apply as above.
