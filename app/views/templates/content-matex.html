Who is entitled to a Maternity Exemption Certificate?

You can apply for a Maternity exemption certificate if you:

  • Are pregnant and have had your pregnancy confirmed by a GP, Midwife or Health Visitor
  • Have given birth within the last 12 months (including a birth of a child that was registered as stillborn), regardless of which country you were living in when you've given birth

A Maternity exemption certificate entitles the holder to free NHS prescriptions within England.
