How do I apply for a PPC?

To apply / re-apply for a Prescription Prepayment Certificate (PPC) you can use one of the following methods:

  • Online - via our website
  • Telephone - Voice Automation line: 0300 330 1341 or 0191 279 0563
  • Pharmacy - registered to sell PPCs on our behalf. There is a list of pharmacies who provide this service on our website
